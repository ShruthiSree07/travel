Frequently asked

What does WanderFive actually do?

You type a location, it returns 5 places worth visiting there, each with a short description — no maps, no bookings, no reviews.

Who is this NOT for?

Anyone who needs live hours, prices, or bookable itineraries — this is a shortlist tool, not a trip-planning suite.

How is this different from just Googling it?

It skips the ad-heavy list pages and gives you exactly 5 curated picks in one search instead of 200 unranked results.

Does it store my searches?

No — WanderFive is fully stateless. Nothing about your search is saved anywhere.
